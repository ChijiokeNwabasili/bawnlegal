Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2025

At Bawn Legal Group, we are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us through our website.

Information We Collect

When you visit our site or fill out our contact form, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Selected legal matter (via dropdown)
  • Any information included in your message

We do not knowingly collect or store sensitive personal data (e.g. health, financial, or government-issued ID numbers) via this website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries or consultation requests
  • Communicate with you regarding your legal needs
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Maintain internal records of communication

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on your consent (when you submit a form), or as necessary to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract for legal services.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it only:

  • With staff or associates bound by confidentiality
  • When required by law or court order
  • To protect our legal rights or prevent fraud

Data Security

While this site does not store data long-term, we take reasonable technical and procedural steps to safeguard any communication we receive from you. However, please note that no transmission over the internet is entirely secure.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or as required by applicable legal or ethical obligations.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing contact@bawnlegal.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing such requests.

Links to Third-Party Sites

Our website may include links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law or our practices. The updated version will always be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
